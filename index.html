Information About Me

Hello, I'm Andrew, a 28-year-old with a profound passion for unraveling the mysteries of the universe. Armed with a Bachelor of Science degree in Applied Physics and a Master of Science in Astronomy and Astrophysics, my academic journey has been a thrilling exploration of the cosmos and its underlying principles.

As someone who finds wonder in both the grandeur of celestial bodies and the intricacies of physical laws, I'm dedicated to sharing the awe-inspiring beauty of astronomy with the world. My academic pursuits have deepened my understanding of the universe, inspiring me to communicate its marvels in engaging and relatable ways.
